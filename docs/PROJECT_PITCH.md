# Project Pitch

## Elevator Pitch
AI Interview Coach is a full-stack solution that turns your resume into a personalized interview practice engine. It generates targeted questions, evaluates your answers, and builds study plans so candidates can prepare smarter, not harder.

## Value Proposition
- Resume-aware interview questions make practice relevant to your background.
- Automated evaluation gives instant feedback so you know where to improve.
- Analytics and study plans help you focus on weaknesses instead of random practice.
- Cross-platform UI makes it easy to practice on mobile or web.

## Why it matters
Many candidates struggle because practice is generic and disconnected from their real experience. By aligning questions with resume highlights and offering AI-driven feedback, this system turns practice into preparation that reflects actual job priorities.

## What sets it apart
- Uses resume content to personalize questions rather than relying on static question banks.
- Integrates generative AI for dynamic follow-ups and adaptive evaluation.
- Includes developer-friendly deployment readiness through Docker and CI.
- Provides a holistic experience: interview practice, performance analytics, and study plan guidance.

## Target user
- Early-to-mid career software engineers preparing for technical interviews
- Candidates who want structured feedback and follow-up practice
- Bootcamp graduates and job seekers targeting high-growth technical roles
- Recruiters or coaches who want to scale candidate preparation resources
